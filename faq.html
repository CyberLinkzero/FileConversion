Frequently Asked Questions

Do you upload my files?

No. Conversions happen locally in your browser. Files never leave your device.

Why is video conversion slower?

Video uses FFmpeg.wasm which runs entirely in your browser. Keep the tab focused and close heavy background apps for best performance.

Maximum file size?

Limited by your device’s memory and the browser. Large files (200MB+) may be slow or fail on low-memory devices.

Which GPS formats do you support?

Import: GPX, KML, TCX, and FIT. Export: CSV/XLSX and back to GPX/KML/TCX from tables.

Is it free?

Yes. The tool is free to use. We may display ads to support hosting and maintenance.

Can I use it offline?

Yes, after the page loads once. Consider “Install as app” via your browser for quick access.

Where is the code?

It’s designed for GitHub Pages. Check our repository readme on the homepage.
