How the tool works

This site performs conversions locally in your browser. Files you select are processed on your device using in-browser libraries (e.g., Canvas, WebAssembly). Your files are not uploaded to our servers.

Information we collect

  • Operational data: Basic, anonymous usage events (e.g., which converter tab was opened) may be measured to improve UX. We do not collect file contents.
  • Logs: Error logs are aggregated without personal file data.

Cookies & third-party services

We may use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver and measure ads.

  • Third parties (including Google) may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits.
  • Users can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Do we upload files?

No. All conversions are client-side. If we add cloud features in the future, they will be opt-in and documented here.

Data retention

We do not store your files. Any optional analytics retain only aggregate metrics.

Your choices

  • Use the tool without uploading files anywhere (default behavior).
  • Control ad personalization via your browser and Google settings.
