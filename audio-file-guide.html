Choosing an Audio Format

Use this:
  • Share anywhere: MP3 (128–192 kbps)
  • Video/streaming: AAC (M4A)
  • Speech + small size: Opus
  • Edit/archive: WAV / FLAC
Notes:
  • Lossy (MP3/AAC/Opus) is not for repeated re-editing.
  • WAV/AIFF are big but perfect for DAW workflows.
